About me

I received the B.S. degree from the Wuhan University of Technology, in 2017, and the M.S. degree from Beijing Institute of Technology, in 2019. I am currently a PhD student with the School of Mechanical Engineering, Beijing Institute of Technology, China. My main research interests include Autonomous Vehicle Safety, Trajectory Planning and Control, Perception validation. Also, I do some research about Computer Vision, such as learning-based objective detection, and Advanced Assistent Driving System, such as human-machine interaction collision avoidance control. I focus on using autonomous driving technology to solve practical problems.
